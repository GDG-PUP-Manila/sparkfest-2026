# **SparkFest 2026** Igniting Innovation, Building Impact, Empowering Communities

## About the Event

SparkFest 2026 is a flagship hackathon organized by Google Developer Groups on Campus Polytechnic University of the Philippines (GDG PUP). This immersive innovation challenge mirrors real-world startup environments, bringing together students from diverse technical, creative, and managerial backgrounds. More than just a competition, SparkFest 2026 cultivates innovation, leadership, collaboration, and social responsibility, empowering students to build solutions that create tangible real-world impact.

## Rationale

SparkFest 2026 bridges the gap between technology and community impact by encouraging students to design solutions that address real societal challenges. Beyond the hackathon, GDG PUP extends its reach through community outreach initiatives that support underserved sectors, including jeepney drivers, security personnel, and local community members.

## Objectives

* Drive innovation through technology-driven solutions.  
* Strengthen collaboration and interdisciplinary teamwork.  
* Provide hands-on hackathon and startup simulation experience.  
* Enhance problem-solving, leadership, and creativity.  
* Foster community engagement within and beyond the university.  
* Connect students with mentors, judges, and industry professionals.

## Venue

**Bulwagang Bonifacio, Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP), Manila.**  
The venue provides an accessible, collaborative environment designed for workshops, coding sessions, pitching activities, networking, and presentations.

## Who Can Join?

SparkFest is open to **college students from all disciplines at PUP and other universities**. Whether you are a **developer, designer, innovator, entrepreneur**, or a **tech enthusiast** looking for your first experience, we welcome you to join us in building impactful solutions.

## Main Activities

* **Hackathon Proper:** Teams build innovative solutions to real-world and community problems.  
* **Mentorship Sessions:** Receive guidance from industry professionals, mentors, and technical experts.  
* **Pitching Day:** Finalist teams present solutions to judges and industry leaders.  
* **Awarding Ceremony:** Recognition for outstanding projects and teams.  
* **Networking Activities:** Connect with students, mentors, and industry partners.  
* **Community Impact Initiative:** Support for selected outreach initiatives benefiting underserved communities. The winning team’s selected community will be the beneficiary of the outreach initiative.
